A parenting library

Five parenting books, made interactive.

Each app distills one book into something you can actually use at 3 a.m. — visualized, quizzed, and searchable. They disagree with each other, and that's the point: read across them and decide for your family.

Your progress is saved on this device. Tap the 🌙 / ☀️ button any time to switch light or dark.
